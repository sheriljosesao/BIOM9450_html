What is ICD-10-AM?

It stands for International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems - Australian Modification. It is a set of codes that are a classification of diseases which stem from the World Health Organisation (WHO) International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD- 10). The ICD-10-AM alongside ACHI (Australian Classification of Health Interventions) and ACS (Australian Coding Standards) are used across both public and private hospitals in Australia to classify episodes of admitted patient care.
